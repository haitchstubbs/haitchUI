# Contributor Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of this open source project, we pledge to foster a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions.
- Provide constructive feedback and accept constructive criticism gracefully.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Help create a collaborative, inclusive, and healthy community.

## AI-Generated Contributions

**AI-generated code or content is not permitted.** All contributions must be authored and reviewed by humans. Submissions identified as AI-generated will be rejected.

## Reporting Issues

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
